UX/UI Design Internship — Task 3

Larder Design System

The shared visual language behind the Larder app (Task 1) and landing page (Task 2) — colour, type, spacing, and a documented component library.

Foundations

Colour

A warm, kitchen-grounded palette: deep herb green carries the brand, amber and rust are reserved for accents and actions, and a soft paper background keeps everything feeling calm rather than clinical.

Components

Buttons

Two variants only. Primary (rust) is reserved for the single most important action on a screen; ghost is for every secondary action. Never use more than one primary button per view.

Form & selection controls

Square checkboxes mark ingredients within a recipe; round checkboxes mark grocery items. The shape difference is deliberate — it signals "part of a recipe" vs. "item to buy."
